Digital Signature

STEPS TO FOLLOW

  1. Have an updated DNA with a chip.
  2. The person must be 18 or older.
  3. Go with the DNA to a National police station to enter the DNI in the dispensed machines, to register a personal password. Example: in Murcia:
  4. Once our password is registered, you need a computer with an internet connection and a DNA card holder.
  5. On our computer, we need a DNA card reader program downloaded.
  6. We go to Google, to open the electronic office of the entity to which we have to process a file.
  7. Once the interested electronic office has been opened and the DNI reader connected by USB to the computer, we enter our DNA in the reader.
  8. We begin the steps to enter the chosen electronic office, we will be asked to give us several options(occasional password or digital certificate...).
  9. We choose the electronic DNI option. Then, it asks us for the password that we put when registering our ID in the dispensing machines of the National Police Station.
  10. Once you have entered the password and give ok, we enter the electronic office of the entity with all data (name, surname, DNA, address).
  11. Follow the steps required by the entity to process a file.
  12. At the end of the process, you will be ask to sign the document digitally.
  13. It will give you the options to verify the entire process and keep a copy of what was processed.
